# Income Statement - User Manual

## Overview
The Income Statement shows your church's financial performance over a period of time - how much income you received, how much you spent, and whether you had a surplus or deficit.

**Location**: Reports â†’ Income Statement  
**Permissions**: Admin, Bookkeeper, Viewer (all users)

## What is an Income Statement?

**Income Statement** = Financial performance report showing:
- **Income**: Money received (donations, offerings, etc.)
- **Expenses**: Money spent (salaries, utilities, etc.)
- **Net Income**: Surplus or deficit (Income - Expenses)

**Formula**: Net Income = Total Income - Total Expenses

**Also Called**: Profit & Loss (P&L), Statement of Activities

## Common Questions

### Q: What time period should I use?
**A**: Depends on your goal:
- **Monthly**: Track progress, catch issues early
- **Quarterly**: Board meetings, trend analysis
- **Annually**: Tax reporting, annual review
- **Custom**: Special reports, mid-year check

### Q: Should I filter by fund?
**A**: 
- **All Funds**: Overall church performance
- **Specific Fund**: Track restricted fund activity
- **Example**: View Building Fund separately to see project income/expenses

### Q: What's the difference from Balance Sheet?
**A**: 
- **Income Statement**: Performance over TIME (month, year)
- **Balance Sheet**: Position at a POINT in time (today, Dec 31)
- **Example**: Income Statement = movie, Balance Sheet = photograph

### Q: Do voided transactions appear?
**A**: No, voided transactions are automatically excluded from all reports.

### Q: Why are in-kind donations shown?
**A**: They're income AND expense, so net effect is usually $0. Shown for transparency.

### Q: Can I export this report?
**A**: Check for "Download" or "Export to PDF/Excel" button (feature may vary by version).

### Q: What if Net Income is negative?
**A**: You have a deficit - spending exceeded income. Review expenses and income immediately.

## Financial Health Indicators

### Healthy Patterns

âœ… **Income > Expenses**: Consistent surplus  
âœ… **Predictable income**: Stable month-to-month  
âœ… **Controlled expenses**: Within budget  
âœ… **Growing giving**: Increasing over time  
âœ… **Balanced spending**: No category dominates

### Warning Signs

ðŸš¨ **Deficit**: Expenses > Income regularly  
ðŸš¨ **Declining giving**: Downward trend  
ðŸš¨ **Expense creep**: Growing faster than income  
ðŸš¨ **Large variances**: Budget way off  
ðŸš¨ **Cash crisis**: Shortfalls becoming common

### Quick Metrics

**Calculate These**:

**Expense Ratio**:
```
Total Expenses / Total Income = ____%
Healthy: 80-90% (leaves 10-20% for savings/reserves)
Warning: >95% (little margin)
Danger: >100% (deficit)
```

**Program Spending**:
```
Ministry Expenses / Total Expenses = ____%
Healthy: 20-30% on programs
Warning: <10% (administrative heavy)
```

**Staff Costs**:
```
Salaries / Total Expenses = ____%
Typical: 40-60%
High: >70% (heavy on staff)
Low: <30% (all volunteer?)
```

## Troubleshooting

### Income/Expenses Don't Match Expectations
**Problem**: Numbers seem wrong  
**Check**:
- Date range selected
- Fund filter (All vs specific)
- Recent transactions recorded
- No duplicate entries

### Report Shows $0 for Everything
**Problem**: All accounts show zero  
**Causes**:
- No transactions in date range
- Wrong fund selected
- Database issue

**Solutions**:
- Verify date range
- Check "All Funds"
- Review Transaction History

### Missing Accounts
**Problem**: Expected accounts don't appear  
**Why**: Accounts with $0 activity don't show  
**Solution**: Normal, only accounts with transactions display

### Can't Compare to Budget
**Problem**: No budget column  
**Why**: Budgets not set up for this period  
**Solution**: Contact admin to set up budgets
